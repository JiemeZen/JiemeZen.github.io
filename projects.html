Doggo v0

Robotic

Low-cost legged Robot dog with 12 joints powered by SG90 servo motors that is controlled by Raspberry Pi/Arduino. Aim to develop simple walking and standing capability.

  • Work in progress

Robo Hand v0

Robotic

Cable-driven robotic hand that is of 1:1 scale to an adult human hand. To design and replicate accurate human-like finger movement. Aim to design a full hand using these fingers.

  • Work in progress

Frontline Fighters

Tech

Retro GBA Game developed in C and assembly language. The program uses button interrupts and game timer running at 10ms for optimal performance. Go on an adventurous journey as a frontline fighter, solving challenging puzzles and battling against viruses across each level, as the difficulty increases.

  • Try the game

Train Solo8 to Walk

Robotic

Use of Deep Reinforcement Learning to teach open source legged robot design Solo 8 to walk on both flat and rough terrain. Mujoco gym is used to emulate the physics to train the agent to walk, it is rewarded for distance and speed, while punished for falls and veering off course.
