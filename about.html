Hi There!

I am a tech enthusiat with strong passion for robotic and innovative design, from Singapore. Loves travelling and reproduce 'what I see thru my eyes' with stills and videos. Financial independence seeker. Avid investor and crypto HODLer. Connect and chat with me on LinkedIn.

I was intrigued by inner workings of devices since young. I played Lego since the age of 3, which helped cultivate my design thinking and ideas. Later I got into Lego Mindstorm Robotic Platform. Participated and won various robotic competitions throughout my educational years. I later majored Aerospace in Polytechnic and Robotics in University. During my time in University, I became Techical lead for NUS Team Bumblebee, where I oversaw the design and development of Bumblebee Autonomous Maritime System. Currently, I am pursuing a career as R&D Engineer at A*STAR, driving robotic and automation solutions for Industrial 4.0.

I pursue lifelong learning as I am always intrigued by new discoveries everyday. As much as a learner, I loves teaching as I am great with breaking down complex problems into simple ideas that could be understood. Therefore, I teach part-time tuitions in the weekends if my time allows.

I also strongly believe in financial independence, and seek to acheive it as soon as possible. I was inspired towards this goal after reading a book by Vicki Robin. My objective is not to retire from work but to seperate personal finance away from work (value creation & pursuing passions).
