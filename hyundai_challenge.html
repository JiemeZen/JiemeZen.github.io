Hyundai-NTU 3DP Challenge 2022

By: Aung Pyei Phyo (Jimmy)


GRID

GRID is a highly modular foldable work desk, which also act as an electronic organizer for cars. It aims to enhance productivity even during commute, while keeping things nice and tidy.

Design Empathy

With built-in power plugs in modern electric vehicle such as Hyundai Ioniq 5, it is intuitive for passengers to use electronic devices. In the modern age with various digital gadgets, we tend to have poor ergonomics as we indulge in countless shows and contents with these devices, while negating proper postures. These causes common occupational hazards such as stiff neck using laptop on laps or hand cramps holding mobile devices. With Grid, user can enjoy their devices at comfort without straining themselves.

Design Features

Grid features a work desk for using laptops or writing on tablets and notebooks. An electronic organizer for mobile electronic devices and items. The work desk is adjustable and can be folded when not in use by simply folding the support stand or removed entirely. This makes Grid an essential accessory to enhance productivity during commute.

Design Idea

The main accessory attachment board is made up of 40x40mm squares, which was inspired by the Ioniq 5’s unique ‘Pixel light’ design. These squares have rods for Grid’s c-clip snap-n-lock mechanism that are found on the accessories, which allows them to be easily attached and removed. Be it phone/tablet holder or a simple hook to hold miscellaneous items, making it highly modular and versatile.
